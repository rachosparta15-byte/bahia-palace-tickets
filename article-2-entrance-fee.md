# Bahia Palace Entrance Fee 2026: Ticket Prices, Discounts & How to Save Money

The official ticket price to enter Bahia Palace is 70 MAD — roughly $7 USD or £5.50. By international standards, that's a bargain for one of the most beautifully preserved Islamic palaces in the world. But the number on the ticket isn't the real cost. The real cost is the 30–45 minutes you spend standing in line at the single ticket booth, in the heat, watching your sightseeing window shrink. This guide breaks down what you'll actually pay, who gets discounts, and whether a skip-the-line ticket is worth it.

---

## Official Entrance Fee

As of 2026, the standard entrance fee set by Morocco's Ministry of Culture is:

| Category | Price |
|----------|-------|
| Adults (foreign visitors) | 70 MAD (~$7 / £5.50 / €6.50) |
| Moroccan nationals | 10 MAD |
| Children under 7 | Free |

Payment at the door is **cash only**. There are no card machines at the ticket window. The nearest ATMs are a short walk away near Rue Riad Zitoun el Kedim, but in a busy medina, "a short walk" can mean 10 extra minutes of navigating alleyways. Come prepared with cash.

The price has been stable for several years and is unlikely to increase significantly in 2026, but always verify with official sources if you're planning well in advance.

---

## Are There Discounts?

### Students

There is no universally applied student discount for foreign visitors. Moroccan students with a valid national student card may access a reduced rate, but international student cards (ISIC and similar) are not reliably honored at the door. Don't count on a discount if you're visiting from abroad.

### Children

Children under 7 enter free. There's no specific signage about this at the entrance — just inform the ticket seller at the window. Children between 7 and 12 are charged the full adult rate of 70 MAD. At that price, a family of four adults plus two young children is looking at roughly 280 MAD for the standard ticket — still inexpensive by any measure.

### Locals vs. Foreign Visitors

Morocco operates a dual pricing system at many state-run cultural sites. Bahia Palace is no exception: Moroccan nationals pay 10 MAD versus 70 MAD for foreign visitors. This is standard practice across the country and consistent with how similar sites are priced across the region.

### Group Discounts

There is no formal group discount at the standard ticket window for foreign visitors. Organized tour groups negotiate rates separately through licensed tour operators. If you're traveling independently, expect to pay 70 MAD per adult regardless of group size.

## Skip-the-Line Ticket: Is It Worth It?

Here's the honest answer: **yes, especially between March and November.**

During Marrakech's peak travel season, the queue at Bahia Palace's single ticket window can stretch to 30–45 minutes. That's time you're standing in the sun, in a narrow street, in what is often very warm weather. Once you finally reach the window, you still need to buy your ticket and potentially wait again before entering.

A [skip-the-line ticket](/en/tickets/skip-the-line) lets you bypass that queue entirely. You show your confirmation at a dedicated entrance and walk straight in.

**When it's absolutely worth it:**
- March through May (spring high season)
- September through October (autumn high season)
- Any public holiday or long weekend
- If you're on a tight schedule and have other sites planned the same day

**When it's less critical:**
- Weekday mornings in January or February
- If you're arriving right at 9:00 AM opening before tour groups arrive

The price difference between a door ticket and a skip-the-line option is modest. The time you save, however, is real — and in a city as rewarding as Marrakech, an extra 40 minutes means another landmark, another souk, or a longer lunch.

## What's Included in the Ticket?

Whether you buy at the door or online, your ticket covers:

- **Full access** to the palace's main courtyards, reception halls, gardens, and the former harem quarters
- Self-guided exploration at your own pace
- Access to all rooms that are currently open to visitors (a small number of rooms are occasionally closed for maintenance or official events)

**Not included:**
- Audio guide (available for hire separately at the entrance)
- Guided tour with a licensed local guide (arranged separately, prices vary)
- Photography permit (standard photography is free and allowed without flash — no extra fee required)

There is no separate premium wing or "VIP access" area at Bahia Palace. The ticket, regardless of how you buy it, covers the same experience.

2. Select your date and number of visitors
3. Complete payment by card (Visa, Mastercard, and major international cards accepted)
4. Receive your confirmation by email — print it or save it to your phone

Booking even 24 hours in advance is enough outside of peak season. During spring and autumn, booking 3–5 days ahead is advisable as time slots can fill up. If you're visiting during Marrakech's busiest weeks (school holidays in the UK, US spring break, French school holidays), book as soon as your trip is confirmed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much does it cost to enter Bahia Palace?**
The standard entrance fee is 70 MAD for foreign visitors (approximately $7 USD / £5.50 / €6.50 as of 2026). Moroccan nationals pay 10 MAD. Children under 7 enter free. Payment at the door is cash only, so bring dirhams.

**Q: Can I get a discount at Bahia Palace with a student card?**
International student cards such as the ISIC are not reliably accepted at Bahia Palace's ticket window. Moroccan nationals with a valid national student card may receive a reduced rate, but foreign visitors should expect to pay the full adult price of 70 MAD regardless of student status.

**Q: Is it cheaper to buy tickets at the door or online?**
The base ticket price is the same. Online [skip-the-line tickets](/en/tickets/skip-the-line) may include a small booking fee, but they save you 30–45 minutes of queuing at the entrance — which in peak season is well worth the marginal difference. During busy periods, online booking also guarantees entry during your chosen time window.
