Capture Stunning Photos at Bahia Palace

The Bahia Palace is one of the most beautiful historical landmarks in Marrakech, Morocco. Famous for its colorful zellige tiles, elegant courtyards, lush gardens, and intricate cedar wood ceilings, the palace offers an extraordinary backdrop for photography.

Whether you are a tourist, travel blogger, influencer, couple, or professional photographer, choosing the right outfit colors can make your photos stand out while complementing the palace's breathtaking architecture.

Best Colors to Wear at Bahia Palace

1. White – Timeless Elegance

White is one of the most popular colors for a photoshoot at Bahia Palace. It creates a beautiful contrast against the vibrant Moroccan mosaics and highlights the palace's architectural details.

Perfect for:

  • Long dresses

  • Linen shirts

  • Traditional Moroccan outfits

  • Couple photography

2. Beige and Neutral Tones – Natural Harmony

Beige, cream, ivory, and sand colors blend perfectly with the palace's historic atmosphere while creating a sophisticated and luxurious look.

Why choose neutral tones?

  • Elegant and timeless appearance

  • Complements Moroccan architecture

  • Creates soft and aesthetic photos

3. Royal Blue – Eye-Catching Contrast

Royal blue stands out beautifully against the warm colors of Bahia Palace and adds depth and sophistication to photographs.

4. Emerald Green – Inspired by the Palace Gardens

The palace's gardens and decorative details pair perfectly with emerald green outfits, creating a refined and exotic look.

5. Burgundy and Deep Red – Royal Style

Rich red and burgundy tones reflect the luxurious history of the palace and create dramatic, memorable photographs.

Colors to Avoid

To achieve the best results, avoid:

  • Neon yellow

  • Fluorescent green

  • Bright orange

  • Overly busy patterns

  • Large logos and distracting graphics

These colors can clash with the palace's artistic details and distract attention from the overall composition.

Best Photo Locations Inside Bahia Palace

The Grand Courtyard

The palace's iconic central courtyard features stunning geometric mosaics and elegant symmetry, making it one of the most photographed locations.

The Andalusian Gardens

Lush greenery, fountains, and peaceful pathways create a romantic atmosphere for portraits and couple photography.

Historic Salons

Decorated ceilings, carved woodwork, and intricate details provide a unique setting for artistic photography.

Traditional Moroccan Doorways

The palace's ornate doors and arches are perfect for capturing authentic Moroccan-inspired photos.

Why Visit Bahia Palace?

Built in the late 19th century, Bahia Palace is one of Marrakech's most important cultural and architectural treasures. Visitors can explore magnificent courtyards, traditional Moroccan craftsmanship, beautiful gardens, and fascinating historical spaces.

A visit to Bahia Palace is a must for anyone looking to discover the rich heritage of Marrakech while capturing unforgettable photographs.
